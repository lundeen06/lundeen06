Hello, I'm Lundeen!

I'm a student at Stanford studying computer science and doing aerospace things - I have tons of interests, but recently have found the most joy working on GNC, AI, satellites, and embedded projects.

**What I'm working on: 🛰️**
* 🛰️ [samwise gnc flight code](https://github.com/stanford-ssi/samwise-adcs-flight) - Attitude Determination & Control System (ADCS) build from the ground up for a 2U Cubesat called SAMWISE. This is a super low-cost bus (<10k/unit) with cameras, reaction wheels, a star tracker, S-band comms, and more. Vertical integration and in-house design lets us bring the cost way down. We'll launch in July or October 2027.
